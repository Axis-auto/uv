How It Works?

Important Safety Warnings:

  • Never point the rays towards eyes. Use protective glasses with UV400+, EN166, or ANSI standards.
  • Do not expose skin to UV rays for extended periods.
  • Do not use the device near water or liquids.
  • Keep the device out of reach of children.

Functions and Uses

  • Paint Inspection: Detects cosmetic paint, objective paint, additional layers, polishing, acrylic, and varnish.
  • Modification Detection: Shows putty and repaired layers, even under PPF or wrapping.
  • Accident and Flood Inspection: Shows water residues, flood marks, or early corrosion and rust.
  • Non-Metal Parts Inspection: Inspects vehicles made of fiberglass and any materials, inspects plastic parts.
  • Interior Upholstery Inspection: Distinguishes if interior upholstery, seats, and doors have been replaced or repainted.

Yes - the device is designed to show material and paint differences even under PPF or protective wraps.
